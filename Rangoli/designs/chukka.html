Chukka Muggu / Pulli Kolam

While sikku kolam is drawn in the gaps between the dots, pulli kolam is drawn on the dots itself. ‘Pulli/Chukka’ means dots. The motifs can be any traditional or even cartoon characters. Flowers, fruits, animals, birds, pots, conch shells are the most common motifs. It is easier than sikku kolam and so practiced widely among children. It looks attractive with many symbolisms.
