Sandhu chukka muggu/Idai pulli kolam

'Sandhu chukka muggu' literally translated as ‘Interlaced dot kolam’. 'Sandhu' means space between any two things and 'Chukka' means dot, here it means the space between dots. This design making involves all kinds of other mentioned designs but the main variation compared to others is in the arrangement of dots. The same distance is maintained between the lines, whereas the dots in consecutive lines are placed in the alternating spaces in the neighbouring line as shown below.
