Muggu is an art form, in which patterns are created on the floor. 'Muggulu'(plural form) is a term used by people from a state called AndhraPradesh, India. Each state uses a different name for the floor art in their own language. Click on the different states in the MAP below to see what a floor art is called in that particular state. I prefer to use word 'Muggulu' or 'Kolam' in my website, as I am from Southern part of India.
