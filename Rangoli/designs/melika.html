Melika Muggu / Sikku Kolam

This design Involves wavy lines in symmetrical patterns enclosing each dot. Sikku/ Melika means ‘tangled’. The design starts from a dot, moves up and down, gets complexly tangled throughout the space between the dots and finally ends in the starting point. This certainly increases creativity. Like solving a rubix cube, drawing this kolam needs concentration. Since this is a complex design, it is better to draw the design in a notebook first.
